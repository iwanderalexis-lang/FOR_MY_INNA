birthday wish

For Inna

for your 21st, and for every day after

Babi,

for your 21st birthday, I just want to wish so many good and gentle things for you.

I hope the universe becomes kinder to you this year. Sana maging mas gentle yung mundo sayo. Sana mas marami kang maranasang days where things feel lighter, where you don’t have to carry so much weight on your shoulders, and where life slowly starts to feel a little softer for you.

I hope that this year allows you to rest in ways you haven’t been able to before. Sana magkaroon ka ng mga moments where you feel truly at peace; the kind where your mind isn’t racing, where your heart feels calm, and where you can just exist without feeling like you always have to fight through something.

I hope you allow yourself to be loved fully. Sana wag mong hadlangan yung mga taong gustong magmahal sayo in the way that you deserve. You deserve a love that is patient with you, gentle with you, and sincere in every way.

I hope you won’t be afraid to be vulnerable. Alam kong sanay ka maging strong and independent. I know you’ve had to be strong for a long time, and I know you’re used to handling things on your own. But I hope you slowly allow yourself to be softer too. I hope you remember that it’s okay to lean on people who genuinely care for you.

I hope you stay healthy in every way that matters. Sana maging mabait sayo yung katawan mo, yung isip mo, at yung puso mo (mging mabait ka rin, DRINK YOUR MEDS MY LOVE). I hope you continue to grow stronger, not just in surviving things, but also in allowing yourself to feel joy, comfort, and peace.

I hope all the aspirations you carry in your heart slowly find their way to you. Sana makuha mo lahat ng pangarap mo sa buhay. The dreams you quietly hold, the goals you work hard for, the life you imagine for yourself. I hope one by one, they start becoming real. I'll be there as you reach them. I promise.

I hope you keep discovering new parts of yourself this year. Sana makita mo lalo how strong, capable, what an amazing person you are, and how much you truly deserve the good things that life has to offer.

And above everything else, I hope you always remember this:

This may be a simple gift, but every part of it was made with you in mind.

Thank you for existing. Thank you for being someone I get to love.

I hope this year becomes one of the gentlest and most beautiful chapters of your life.

Mahal kita, Inna. Lagi't lagi.

Happy 21st birthday, mahal ko. ♡

— forever yours, lex
